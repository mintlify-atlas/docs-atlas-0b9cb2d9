BuilderPulse publishes a daily intelligence report for indie hackers and builders — answering 20 structured questions every morning about what launched, what's trending, what developers are complaining about, and what you should build today. Reports are available in English and Chinese, delivered via GitHub and RSS.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Star and watch the repository">
    Go to [github.com/BuilderPulse/BuilderPulse](https://github.com/BuilderPulse/BuilderPulse) and click **Watch → All Activity** to get notified of every new report.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe via RSS">
    Add the Atom feed to your RSS reader: `https://github.com/BuilderPulse/BuilderPulse/commits/main.atom` — every daily report appears as a new commit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read today's report">
    Open the latest English report at `en/YYYY/YYYY-MM-DD.md` or the Chinese edition at `zh/YYYY/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Act on the recommendations">
    Jump to the **Action** section of each report for specific build recommendations with validated demand signals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
