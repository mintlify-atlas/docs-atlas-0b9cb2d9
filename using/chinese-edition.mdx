BuilderPulse publishes a full Chinese-language edition alongside every English report. The Chinese edition — known as 中文版 — is not a translation. It covers the same 20 structured questions and draws from the same 300+ signals across Hacker News, GitHub, Product Hunt, HuggingFace, Google Trends, and Reddit, but it is written for a Chinese-speaking builder audience, with framing and emphasis suited to that context. Both editions publish daily, timestamped to Shanghai time.

## File path format

Chinese edition reports live in the `zh/` directory of the repository, organized by year:

```text
zh/YYYY/YYYY-MM-DD.md
```

For example, the April 16, 2026 Chinese report is at:

```text
zh/2026/2026-04-16.md
```

You can browse the full Chinese archive at [`zh/`](https://github.com/BuilderPulse/BuilderPulse/tree/main/zh) in the repository.

## How it differs from a translation

The Chinese edition is written from scratch in Chinese, not machine-translated from the English report. While both editions answer the same 20 questions using the same underlying data, the Chinese edition:

- Uses phrasing and framing natural to a Chinese-speaking builder audience
- Adds inline contextual notes in parentheses for cultural or situational clarity (for example: `（数据找不回来了）` appended after a quote about unrecoverable files)
- Structures takeaways for builders who may be working in Chinese-language markets or with different regulatory contexts

This means the two editions can be read independently — you don't need to cross-reference the English version to understand the Chinese one.

## Report structure

The Chinese edition follows the same six-section, 20-question structure as the English edition:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="发现机会 (Discovery)">
    - 本周有哪些独立开发者产品发布？ — Which solo-founder products launched?
    - 哪些搜索词本周出现异常飙升？ — Which search terms are surging?
    - GitHub 上哪些快速增长的开源项目尚无商业化版本？ — Which fast-growing open-source projects lack a commercial version?
    - 开发者在抱怨哪些工具？ — What tools are developers complaining about?
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="技术选型 (Tech Radar)">
    - 本周有哪些主要公司关闭或降级了产品？ — Did any major company shut down or downgrade a product?
    - 本周增长最快的开发者工具是什么？ — What are the fastest-growing developer tools?
    - HuggingFace 上最热门的模型是什么？ — What are the hottest HuggingFace models?
    - 本周最重要的开源 AI 进展是什么？ — What are the most important open-source AI developments?
    - 本周最热门的 Show HN 项目使用了哪些技术栈？ — What tech stacks are Show HN projects using?
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="竞争情报 (Competitive Intel)">
    - 独立开发者在讨论哪些收入和定价问题？ — What revenue and pricing discussions are happening?
    - 有哪些沉寂已久的旧项目突然复活？ — Are any dormant old projects reviving?
    - 本周有没有"XX 已死"或迁移类文章？ — Are there any "XX is dead" or migration articles?
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="趋势判断 (Trends)">
    - 本周最频繁的技术关键词是什么？ — What are the most frequent tech keywords?
    - VC 和 YC 目前在关注什么方向？ — What are VCs and YC focusing on?
    - 哪些 AI 搜索词正在降温？ — Which AI search terms are cooling off?
    - 新词雷达：哪些全新概念正在从零崛起？ — Which brand-new concepts are rising from zero?
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="行动触发 (Action)">
    - 用今天的 2 小时或一整个周末，我应该做什么？ — What should I build today?
    - 哪些定价和商业化模式值得研究？ — What pricing and monetization models are worth studying?
    - 今日最反直觉的发现是什么？ — What is today's most counter-intuitive finding?
    - Product Hunt 产品与开发者工具在哪里重叠？ — Where do Product Hunt products overlap with dev tools?
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
